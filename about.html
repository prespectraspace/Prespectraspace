About Prespectra Space

Pioneering the Next Generation of 3D Spatial Intelligence

At Prespectra, we bridge the gap between physical spaces and the digital universe. Using state-of-the-art LiDAR, photogrammetry, and spatial computing, we create ultra-precise, millimeter-accurate digital twins of premium real estate, complex commercial environments, and industrial facilities.

Our mission is to empower decision-makers, marketers, and developers with immersive virtual environments that look stunning, feel realistic, and contain actionable spatial intelligence.
